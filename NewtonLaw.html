牛顿定律 Newton's Laws of Motion

第一定律 (First Law)

一切物体在没有受到力的作用时，总保持静止状态或匀速直线运动状态。

An object remain at rest or uniform motion unless acted by a resultant force

物体的加速度与所受合外力成正比，与物体的质量成反比。

The acceleration of an object is proportional to the resultant force and inversely proportional to its mass.
